💡Tips & Information 提示
Integrated Shield Plans (ISP): MediShield Life only covers Class B2/C wards. For Class B1/A/Private ward coverage, consider adding an ISP from AIA, Great Eastern, NTUC Income, Prudential, or Singlife. ISPs are paid from MediSave + cash.
MediSave Usage: You can pay your MediShield Life premium and part of your hospital bills from your MediSave account. The withdrawal limit is $450-$900/day depending on the type of treatment.
