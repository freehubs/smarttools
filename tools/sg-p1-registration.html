✅Preparation Checklist 准备清单
  • Verify home-school distance on OneMap School Query (onemap.gov.sg) 确认住家到学校距离
  • Ensure SingPass login works for online registration 确保 SingPass 登录正常
  • Prepare child's birth certificate and immunization records 准备出生证明和疫苗记录
  • If Phase 2A: confirm alumni membership / MOE KG enrolment / staff status 确认校友会/MOE幼儿园/职员身份
  • If Phase 2B: complete 40 hours of parent volunteering before 1 Jul 在7月1日前完成40小时义工
  • Check if target school was oversubscribed in previous years (KiasuParents forum) 查看目标学校往年超额情况
  • Have backup school choices ready in case of balloting 准备备选学校
  • For PR children: be aware PR applicants ballot separately after SC 永久居民单独抽签
