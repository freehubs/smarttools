Tips for Better Rates 汇款省钱技巧
  • Transfer larger amounts to reduce per-unit fee impact 大额转账可摊薄手续费
  • Use PayNow / bank transfer to fund (avoid card fees) 使用 PayNow 充值，避免信用卡手续费
  • Check for first-transfer promotions 查看首次汇款优惠（Wise/Instarem 常有）
  • Compare rates at transfer time — they change throughout the day 汇率实时变动，转账前再比较一次
  • Instarem often offers zero-fee transfers with competitive rates Instarem 经常免手续费且汇率有竞争力
  • Wise uses mid-market rate but charges a transparent fee Wise 使用中间汇率但收取透明手续费
